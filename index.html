About

I'm Michelle, a soon-to-graduate undergraduate student at Barnard College at Columbia University, NYC. I'm also a software engineering intern at the ride-app startup Whisk, where I've learned to love the code review (along with a lot of other pretty cool stuff). I'm always ready for projects that need not just an engineer, but a designer, creative thinker, and team player.

When I'm not imagining or engineering awesome things, I enjoy holding or otherwise being in the presence of cats, playing the occasional video game, catching up on an academic year's worth of missed anime series, and reading the latest Brian Sanderson novels on my Kindle. As one might expect, I also suffer from the occasional Vitamin D deficiency.
